What Makes our Teaching Style Stand Out

Our app is intriguing to teens because we have created a system to help teens become financially stable using their own phone, no hassle with pen and paper anymore!

Our app is completely customizable for the teens!

We are not like the other mobile banking apps because we do not expect our users to come with knowledge of how to budget and save. We also do not include the advanced and overwhelming aspects for the teens like investments, credit scores, and loans because our users are not yet at that stag of their life.

Our tracking system allows users to follow trends, which inreturn avoids debt or running out of money.
